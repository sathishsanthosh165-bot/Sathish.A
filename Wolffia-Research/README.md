# Wolffia Research Project

## Overview

Wolffia (Duckweed) is one of the world's smallest flowering plants and is recognized for its high protein content, rapid growth rate, and potential applications in sustainable food systems.

This project focuses on understanding Wolffia cultivation, biomass production, nutritional value, and potential applications in food and agricultural industries.

---

## Research Objectives

- Study growth characteristics of Wolffia species
- Evaluate cultivation methods and environmental requirements
- Explore protein-rich food applications
- Investigate large-scale production potential
- Assess suitability for sustainable agriculture systems

---

## Areas of Interest

### Cultivation
